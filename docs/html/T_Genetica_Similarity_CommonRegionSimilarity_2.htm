CommonRegionSimilarityTProgram, TOutput Class

Measures the similarity of two ITreeProgramTOutput based on the common-region of their expressions' trees. Common-region similarity is given by the division of the number of sub-programs in the common-region and the max number of sub-programs between the two given programs.
